Pizza

Ingredients and preparation steps

The best pizza recipe there is!

Ingredients

  • 5 dl Flour
  • 1 package of Yeast
  • 1 liter of Water
  • 1 tea spoon of Salt
  • 3 table spoons of Olive oil
  • 1 can of tomato sauce

Preparation steps

  1. mix the flour, water, yeast, salt and oil to make a dough
  2. roll the dough flat
  3. pour some tomato sauce on top of the dough
  4. add ingredients to your liking
  5. bake in oven at 225 celsius for 10 minutes
