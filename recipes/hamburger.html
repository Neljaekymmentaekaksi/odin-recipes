Hamburger

Ingredients and preparation steps

The best hamburger recipe there is!

Ingredients

  • 4 hamburger buns
  • 400 g of minced meat
  • lettuce
  • 2 tomatoes
  • pickles
  • Ketchups

Preparation steps

  1. Cut the buns in half
  2. make 4 hamburger steaks from the meat and fry it
  3. pile lettuce, tomato slices, pickles and ketchup on each bottom half of a hamburger bun and put the ¨hat¨ on
  4. Enjoy!
