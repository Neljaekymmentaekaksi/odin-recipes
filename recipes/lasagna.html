Lasagna

Ingredients and preparation steps

The best lasagna recipe there is!

Ingredients

  • 1 package of lasagne pasta sheets
  • tomato sauce
  • 300g cheese
  • 1 liter of milk
  • 1 tea spoon of salt
  • 1 can of beans
  • 400g of minced meat
  • Handful of fresh basil

Preparation steps

  1. Prepare the sauces
  2. cook the meat
  3. Layer the sauces, pasta and meat into the oven dish
  4. cook in the oven at 225 celsius for 1 hour
